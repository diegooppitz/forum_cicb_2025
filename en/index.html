Leather chain: overview, evolution and best practices

with Ricardo Andrade, Technical and Sustainability Advisor at CICB

An experienced global business leader with a career developed in the beef and leather sectors, Ricardo will bring the latest figures and sector developments.

Minerva Leather Presentation

with Otavio Klein, Executive Manager at Minerva Leather

A graduate in International trade from Unisinos and with an MBA in Business Management from FGV, he has over 15 years of experience in the leather sector. Since 2015 at Minerva, he is responsible for the Commercial, Sustainability, Logistics, and Business Intelligence sectors.

IDH Institutional Presentation

IDH is a private foundation that works with producers, companies, financiers, governments and civil society to promote sustainable trade in global value chains.

A proposal for extending animal traceability to leather based on NBR 15006

with André Luiz Tessele Nodari, electrical engineer from UFRGS, founding partner of NBN Tecnologia, counselor at Abrameq

The presentation will be given by André Luiz Tessele Nodari, electrical engineer from UFRGS. He is a founding partner of NBN Tecnologia Ltda. He was president of Abrameq and counselor at Abqtic. He currently participates on the board of Abrameq.

Panel: Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock and the Sustainability

with Carlos Goulart, Secretary of Agricultural Defense (SDA) at the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAPA), and Renata Miranda, Secretary of Innovation, Sustainable Development, Irrigation, and Cooperativism (SDI) at MAPA.

Projects, updates, and perspectives on the topic of sustainability and traceability from the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock.

Panel: the future of the sector and traceability

with Francisco Pugliese de Castro (CNA); Fernando Sampaio (Abiec); Carlos Goulart (MAPA/SDA); Renata Miranda (MAPA/SDI); José Fernando Bello (moderator/CICB).

A moment for the different links in the production chain to share ideas and predictions on the topic.
